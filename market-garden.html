Market Gardening Project

Overview

I operate a small-scale market gardening enterprise focused on the production of high-quality fruits using ecologically sound practices. The project is entirely self-managed and combines practical horticulture, long-term planning, and close observation of soil–plant interactions.

Focus & Practices

  • Small-scale fruit production with an emphasis on quality over yield
  • Soil health–oriented management and minimal external inputs
  • Manual and low-mechanization approaches
  • Direct responsibility for planning, maintenance, and harvest
